Part A

My Initial Ideation

In the first accessment, I started researching the old pictures of Queensland, including the old maps and photos. This helps me to brainstorm any relative features these datasets might be able to provide.

And indeed, it turns out that these datasets contain a lot of useful information, especially the old maps, which record a lot of information about the roads and places in Queensland.

Forming Team: 8 A.M.

After a tricky start, we finally formed our team, and started to discuss as a team what we should do. We had a lot of ideas, and after a long discussion, we decided to focus on the 3 datasets:

  • The Bikeway Sections
    •
  • The Drinking Fountains
    •
  • The Events
    •

    Therefore, these datasets have brought us a lot of inspiration for our project, including the 3 main features:

    Bike lane explorer


    Interactive map displaying bike lane information and tap water locations. We use three distincitve datasets and APIs to retrieve data and display -> bike lane and tap water in map, event in event page and bike lane page

    Advanced search filter


    Empowering user choices in bike lane exploration tailored to users' needs and preferences.

    Share experiences by review


    Enable bikers to share valuable feedback and biking experiences with others who share similar interests, fostering an interactive and supportive community.
